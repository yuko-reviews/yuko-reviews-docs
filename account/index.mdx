## Account Overview

The Account section is where you configure the workspace-level settings that apply across every review, email, and widget Yuko sends on your behalf. Most of these are set once during onboarding and updated only when your team or brand changes.

### **Navigation**

Yuko Dashboard → **Settings → Account**

---

## What You Can Manage

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Account Settings" icon="gear" href="/account/account-settings">
    Timezone, date and time format, sender name, reply-to email, and default logo. These feed directly into every email Yuko sends.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Updating Logo" icon="image" href="/account/updating-logo">
    Upload or replace the logo shown in review request emails, review forms, and widget headers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Store Management" icon="buildings" href="/account/multi-store-management">
    Manage multiple stores or brands under a single Yuko account. Useful for agencies and merchants running more than one storefront.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Security & Access" icon="lock" href="/account/security-and-access">
    Control roles and permissions so each team member only has the access they need.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Members" icon="users" href="/account/members">
    Invite teammates, assign roles, and remove access when people leave.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
