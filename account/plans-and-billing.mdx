## Order-based pricing, explained

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What counts as an order?" icon="receipt" defaultOpen>
    Yuko counts an order when it reaches a **completed / fulfilled** status in WooCommerce. Cancelled, refunded, and failed orders do not consume your order quota.

    Your quota resets on the first day of each billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I go over my included orders?" icon="gauge-high">
    On paid plans, overage is billed at **$5 per additional 100 orders**. Yuko never stops sending review requests for exceeding the quota — your review flow continues uninterrupted.

    On the Free plan, orders beyond 50 per month are queued. Upgrade to continue review collection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans at any time?" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

    Cancelling reverts your account to the Free plan. You keep all your collected reviews.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer annual billing?" icon="calendar">
    Annual billing with a discount is available on Advanced and Growth plans. [Contact sales](https://cal.com/rameshelamathi/yuko-demo) to set it up.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Manage your subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing" icon="credit-card">
    In the Yuko Dashboard, click **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review current usage" icon="chart-column">
    The top card shows your plan, the current order count, and renewal date. Use this to gauge when to upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change plan" icon="arrow-up-right-dots">
    Pick a plan card and click **Select Plan**. You'll be redirected to our payment provider to confirm the change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update payment method or invoices" icon="file-invoice-dollar">
    Click **Manage Subscription** on the current subscription card to update card details, download invoices, or cancel.
  </Step>
</Steps>
