---
title: "List customer rewards"
---

Retrieves a list of customer rewards. Commonly used to display a customer's issued coupons, discounts, or store credits.

**GET /customer-rewards**

#### **Authorizations**

**Authorization**

**string**

**header**

**required**

Bearer authentication header of the form `Bearer <token>`, where `<token>` is your auth token.

#### **Query Parameters**

**customer_uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

Filter results to only rewards belonging to the specified customer.

Example:

`"a02a4ad4-22c3-4f46-95fb-497e10f3e7d3"`

**status**

**enum\<string\>**

Filter results to only rewards matching the provided status.

Available options:

`active`,

`used`,

`inactive`,

`expired`,

`deleted`,

`revert`

**source_type**

**enum\<string\>**

Filter results to only rewards from the specified source.

Available options:

`loyalty_rewards`,

`loyalty_tier_benefits`,

`loyalty_campaigns`,

`users`,

`external`

**updated_at_min**

**string\<date-time\>**

Filter results to only rewards updated after the provided date and time.

Example:

`"2025-12-01T00:00:00.000Z"`

**limit**

**integer**

**default: 50**

The maximum number of customer rewards to retrieve.

Required range: `1 <= x <= 250`

**cursor**

**string**

Cursor for the page of results to retrieve. Omit to retrieve the first page.

**200 - application/json**
