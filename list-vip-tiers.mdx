---
title: "List VIP tiers"
---

Retrieves a list of VIP tiers for your organisation, sorted by `threshold_value` in ascending order.

**GET /tiers**

#### **Authorizations**

**Authorization**

**string**

**header**

**required**

Bearer authentication header of the form `Bearer <token>`, where `<token>` is your auth token.

#### **Response**

**200 - application/json**

**tiers**

**object[]**

Hide child attributes

**tiers.id**

**integer**

Unique integer identifier for the VIP tier.

Example:

`1`

**tiers.uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

Unique UUID identifier for the VIP tier.

Example:

`"7fb39c85-3a3f-4247-8333-2e05a9c8ae28"`

**tiers.org_uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

The UUID of the organisation this tier belongs to.

Example:

`"ed39de7b-6c31-46bc-af56-ef51676a08e0"`

**tiers.name**

**string**

The display name of the VIP tier.

Example:

`"Silver"`

**tiers.description**

**string | null**

A description of the VIP tier shown to customers.

Example:

`null`

**tiers.identifier**

**string**

A unique slug-style identifier for the tier, auto-generated from the tier name.

Example:

`"SILVER"`

**tiers.threshold_value**

**integer**

The points value a customer must accumulate to reach this tier.

Example:

`0`

**tiers.multiplier**

**number**

The points earning multiplier applied to customers in this tier.

Example:

`1`

**tiers.is_default**

**boolean**

Whether this is the default tier assigned to customers who have not yet reached any threshold.

Example:

`false`

**tiers.benefits**

**object[]**

A list of benefits associated with this tier.

Hide child attributes

**tiers.benefits.uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

Unique identifier for the benefit.

Example:

`"b1c2d3e4-f5a6-7890-bcde-f01234567890"`

**tiers.benefits.tier_uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

The UUID of the tier this benefit belongs to.

Example:

`"7fb39c85-3a3f-4247-8333-2e05a9c8ae28"`

**tiers.benefits.name**

**string**

The display name of the benefit.

Example:

`"10% Discount on entry"`

**tiers.benefits.benefit_type**

**string**

The type of benefit issued to the customer.

Example:

`"fixed_discount"`

**tiers.benefits.benefit_value**

**object**

The value configuration for this benefit. Structure varies by `benefit_type`.

Show child attributes

**tiers.benefits.benefit_value.value**

**number**

The discount amount or percentage depending on `benefit_type`.

Example:

`10`

**tiers.benefits.benefit_value.points_required**

**integer**

Points required to redeem this benefit, if applicable.

Example:

`0`

**tiers.benefits.platform_discount_id**

**string | null**

The discount identifier in the external ecommerce platform for this benefit.

Example:

`"1121797341367"`

**tiers.benefits.ordering**

**integer**

The sort order of the benefit within the tier.

Example:

`0`

**tiers.benefits.metadata**

**object | null**

Additional metadata for the benefit.

Example:

`null`

**tiers.benefits.created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the benefit was created.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**tiers.benefits.updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the benefit was last updated.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**tiers.metadata**

**object | null**

Additional metadata for the tier.

Example:

`null`

**tiers.created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the tier was created.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**tiers.updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the tier was last updated.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**tiers.deleted_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time when the tier was deleted. `null` if not deleted.

Example:

`null`
