---
title: "List VIP tiers"
---

**GET** / **vip_tiers - Try it**

This endpoint requires the **vip_tier:read** scope.

Results are sorted by `milestone` in ascending order. This endpoint does not support pagination of any kind.

#### **Authorizations**

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#authorization-authorization)

**Authorization**

**string**

**header**

**required**

Bearer authentication header of the form `Bearer <token>`, where `<token>` is your auth token.

#### **Query Parameters**

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#parameter-include)

**include**

**enum\<string\>**

A comma-separated list of nested objects to include in the response.

Available options:

`perks`, 

`entry_rewards` 

#### **Response**

**200 - application/json**

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers)

**vip_tiers**

**object[]**

Hide child attributes

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-id)

**vip_tiers.id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the VIP tier.

Example:

`426715799`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-name)

**vip_tiers.name**

**string**

The display name of the VIP tier.

Example:

`"Gold"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-image-url)

**vip_tiers.image_url**

**string**

The image of the VIP tier.

Example:

`"https://platform.smile.io/v1/images/vip_tiers/vip-tier-3.svg"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-milestone)

**vip_tiers.milestone**

**integer**

The threshold that a customer must reach to be eligible for the VIP tier. Represents either an amount of points or a dollar amount, depending on the loyalty program's configuration.

Example:

`500`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-perks)

**vip_tiers.perks**

**object[]**

A set of nested objects representing the ongoing benefits a customer is entitled to when they are in the VIP tier.

Hide child attributes

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-perks-items-name)

**vip_tiers.perks.name**

**string**

A description of the benefit.

Example:

`"Early access to new product releases"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-entry-rewards)

**vip_tiers.entry_rewards**

**object[]**

A set of nested Reward objects representing the rewards that are issued to a customer when they enter the VIP tier.

Hide child attributes

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-entry-rewards-items-id)

**vip_tiers.entry_rewards.id**

**number**

Unique identifier for the reward.

Example:

`924565472`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-entry-rewards-items-name)

**vip_tiers.entry_rewards.name**

**string**

The name of the reward.

Example:

`"Order discount"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-entry-rewards-items-description)

**vip_tiers.entry_rewards.description**

**string**

A description of the reward.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-entry-rewards-items-image-url)

**vip_tiers.entry_rewards.image_url**

**string**

An image for the reward.

Example:

`"https://platform-images.smilecdn.co/3755938.png"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-entry-rewards-items-created-at)

**vip_tiers.entry_rewards.created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward was created.

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/list-vip-tiers#response-vip-tiers-items-entry-rewards-items-updated-at)

**vip_tiers.entry_rewards.updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward was last updated.

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`
