<Info>

  - **Layout** — choose how reviews are arranged
  - **Style** — typography, spacing, borders
  - **Colors** — match your brand palette
  - **Preferences** — filters, sort order, what to show
  - **Custom CSS** — advanced overrides for developers
  - **Code integration** — shortcode and block embed options
