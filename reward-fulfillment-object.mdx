---
title: "Reward Fulfillment object"
---

A reward fulfillment is a reward (e.g. discount code) has been issued to a customer. Reward fulfillments cannot be directly created, they are automatically generated by the system based on the merchant’s loyalty program configuration when an [**activity is created**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/activities/create-activity) or a [**points product is purchased**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-products/purchase-points-product).

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-id)

**id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`625478984`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-name)

**name**

**string**

The name of the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`"$5 off coupon"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-code)

**code**

**string**

A unique code for the customer to use or apply the reward fulfillment. This is commonly a discount code the customer applies at checkout on their next order, but it can be a unique code they use for other purposes like accessing an exclusive page on the merchant's website.

Example:

`"5off-e26d02e39149"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-customer-id)

**customer_id**

**string**

The ID of the customer that the reward fulfillment belongs to.

Example:

`"304169228"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-fulfillment-status)

**fulfillment_status**

**enum\<string\>**

The fulfillment status of the reward fulfillment, indicating whether the reward is available to the customer.

Available options:

`pending`, 

`issued`, 

`cancelled`, 

`failed` 

Example:

`"issued"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-image-url)

**image_url**

**string**

An image for the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`"https://platform-images.smilecdn.co/9283449.png"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-action-text-one-of-0)

**action_text**

**string | null**

A short call-to-action style text, appropriate for use in buttons or links, guiding the customer on how to use the reward fulfillment. This field may be blank

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-action-url-one-of-0)

**action_url**

**string | null**

A URL destination guiding the customer on how to use the reward, often paired with the `action_text` for use in buttons or links. This field may be blank.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-usage-instructions-one-of-0)

**usage_instructions**

**string | null**

A description of how the customer can use or apply the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`"Use this discount code on your next order!"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-terms-and-conditions-one-of-0)

**terms_and_conditions**

**string | null**

A description of the reward fulfillment's limitations or restrictions. When the reward is a discount code, this commonly includes expiry date, minimum order amounts, or applicability to specific product collections.

Example:

`"Reward expires on June 7, 2025. Reward can only be used on purchase of $50 or more. Reward can only be used on select product collections."`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-expires-at-one-of-0)

**expires_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment expires. When the reward is a discount code, this refers to the expiry date of the discount, and a `null` value indicates that the discount code does not expire.

Example:

`"2025-06-07T23:59:59.999Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-usage-status)

**usage_status**

**enum\<string\>**

The usage status of the reward fulfillment, indicating whether the generated reward has been used by the customer.

Available options:

`used`, 

`unused`, 

`untracked` 

Example:

`"unused"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-used-at-one-of-0)

**used_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment was used by the customer. If the reward fulfillments does not support usage tracking, this field may be `null` even after the reward fulfillment has been used.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-created-at)

**created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment was created.

Example:

`"2024-12-07T20:15:27.893Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/reward-fulfillments/reward-fulfillment-object#schema-updated-at)

**updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment was last updated.

Example:

`"2024-12-07T20:15:27.893Z"`
