---
title: "Way to Earn Object"
---

A way to earn (campaign) represents an action a customer can complete to earn loyalty points. Yuko supports a variety of trigger events such as placing an order, leaving a review, signing up, or following on social media.

**uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

Unique identifier for the campaign in Yuko.

Example:

`"a1b2c3d4-e5f6-7890-abcd-ef1234567890"`

**org_uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

The UUID of the organisation this campaign belongs to.

Example:

`"7789dd6a-5f90-4ad1-9126-63ada4827f79"`

**name**

**string**

The display name of the way to earn.

Example:

`"Place an order"`

**description**

**string | null**

A description of the way to earn, used for display purposes.

Example:

`"Earn points every time you place an order."`

**trigger_event**

**enum\<string\>**

The action that triggers the campaign and awards points to the customer.

Available options:

`purchase`,

`signup`,

`review`,

`daily_visit`,

`birthday`,

`anniversary`,

`twitter_follow`,

`twitter_share`,

`instagram_follow`,

`instagram_share`,

`facebook_like`,

`facebook_share`,

`newsletter_subscribe`

Example:

`"purchase"`

**earning_rule**

**object | null**

Defines how points are calculated when the campaign is triggered.

Show child attributes

**earning_rule.type**

**enum\<string\>**

The method used to calculate the points awarded.

Available options:

`fixed`,

`variable`

Example:

`"variable"`

**earning_rule.value**

**number**

The number of points awarded. For `variable` type, this is the points earned per `per_amount` unit.

Example:

`5`

**earning_rule.per_amount**

**number | null**

Only present when `type` is `variable`. The unit amount required to earn the specified `value` of points (e.g. `1` means 5 points per $1 spent).

Example:

`1`

**conditions**

**object | null**

Optional conditions that must be met for the campaign to trigger. May include minimum order amounts, eligible product categories, or customer segments.

Example:

`null`

**metadata**

**object | null**

Additional metadata for the campaign such as display settings, tracking codes, or notification preferences.

Example:

`null`

**ordering**

**integer**

The sort order of the campaign as displayed to customers.

Example:

`1`

**is_active**

**boolean**

Whether the campaign is currently active and able to award points.

Example:

`true`

**created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the campaign was created.

Example:

`"2026-03-09T14:02:06.000Z"`

**updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the campaign was last updated.

Example:

`"2026-03-09T14:02:06.000Z"`

**deleted_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time when the campaign was deleted. `null` if not deleted.

Example:

`null`
