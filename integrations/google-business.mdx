## What this integration does

- **Imports your Google reviews** into Yuko's Manage Reviews dashboard
- **Surfaces new Google reviews** in your daily moderation workflow alongside Yuko-collected reviews
- **Syncs merchant replies** — type a reply in Yuko, it posts to Google Business Profile
- **Tags imported reviews** with `source: google` so you can filter them out of on-site widgets

This integration does **not** push Yuko product reviews to Google Business Profile — Google doesn't accept third-party reviews on business listings. For search-result star ratings, use [Google SEO rich snippets](/integrations/google-seo) and [Google Shopping feed](/integrations/google-shopping) instead.

## Connect Google Business Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Connect" icon="plug">
    In Yuko: **Account → Integrations → Google Business Profile → Connect with Google**.

    You'll be redirected to Google to authorize Yuko.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant the required scopes" icon="check">
    Yuko requests:
    - **Read and manage business.profile reviews**
    - **Read business profile location info**

    Approve — these are the minimum scopes needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the location to sync" icon="location-dot">
    If your Google account manages multiple business locations, pick the one that matches the store connected to Yuko.

    Multi-location syncing is available on request — [contact support](https://yukoreviews.com/support).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the initial import" icon="hourglass">
    Yuko imports the last 200 Google reviews. Large accounts may take 5–10 minutes. New reviews appear in near-real-time after that.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Google reviews in Yuko

Imported Google reviews appear in **Reviews → Manage Reviews** tagged with a **Google** badge.

- Replies you post via the reply box are sent to Google Business Profile immediately
- Yuko does **not** change the status of Google reviews (no approve/reject) — Google publishes all reviews by default
- You cannot delete Google reviews from Yuko; use Google's own flagging flow for that
- The star rating on Google reviews is imported as-is

---

## Disconnect

**Account → Integrations → Google Business Profile → Disconnect**.

Imported reviews remain in your Yuko dashboard as a historical record. To remove them entirely, delete each review manually after disconnecting.
