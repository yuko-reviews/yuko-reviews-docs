---
title: "Purchase a points product"
---

Exchanges a customer’s points for a reward by purchasing a points product.

**POST**

/

**points_products**

/

**{id}**

/

**purchase**

**Try it**

This endpoint requires the **points_purchase:write** scope.

#### **Authorizations**

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-products/purchase-points-product#authorization-authorization)

**Authorization**

**string**

**header**

**required**

Bearer authentication header of the form `Bearer <token>`, where `<token>` is your auth token.

#### **Path Parameters**

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-products/purchase-points-product#parameter-id)

**id**

**integer**

**required**

ID of the points product to purchase.

Example:

`132456921`

#### **Body**

**application/json**

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-products/purchase-points-product#body-customer-id)

**customer_id**

**integer**

**required**

ID of the customer who is purchasing the points product.

Example:

`304169228`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-products/purchase-points-product#body-points-to-spend)

**points_to_spend**

**integer**

The number of points that will be spent on behalf of the customer. Only applies when purchasing a points product whose `exchange_type` is `variable`, otherwise should be left blank.

Example:

`500`

#### **Response**

**201 - application/json**

The points product was successfully purchased.
