---
title: "Points Purchase object"
---

A record of the customer exchanging their points for a reward. Points purchases cannot be directly created, they are automatically generated by the system when a customer [**purchases a points product**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-products/purchase-points-product).

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-id)

**id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the points purchase.

Example:

`665523890`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-customer-id)

**customer_id**

**integer**

The ID of the customer who performed the points purchase.

Example:

`304169228`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-points-product-id)

**points_product_id**

**integer**

The ID of the points product that was purchased.

Example:

`132456921`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-points-spent)

**points_spent**

**integer**

The total points spent by the customer.

Required range: `x >= 0`

Example:

`500`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment)

**reward_fulfillment**

**object**

A record of a reward that was issued to a customer.

Hide child attributes

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-id)

**reward_fulfillment.id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`625478984`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-name)

**reward_fulfillment.name**

**string**

The name of the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`"$5 off coupon"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-code)

**reward_fulfillment.code**

**string**

A unique code for the customer to use or apply the reward fulfillment. This is commonly a discount code the customer applies at checkout on their next order, but it can be a unique code they use for other purposes like accessing an exclusive page on the merchant's website.

Example:

`"5off-e26d02e39149"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-customer-id)

**reward_fulfillment.customer_id**

**string**

The ID of the customer that the reward fulfillment belongs to.

Example:

`"304169228"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-fulfillment-status)

**reward_fulfillment.fulfillment_status**

**enum\<string\>**

The fulfillment status of the reward fulfillment, indicating whether the reward is available to the customer.

Available options:

`pending`, 

`issued`, 

`cancelled`, 

`failed` 

Example:

`"issued"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-image-url)

**reward_fulfillment.image_url**

**string**

An image for the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`"https://platform-images.smilecdn.co/9283449.png"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-action-text-one-of-0)

**reward_fulfillment.action_text**

**string | null**

A short call-to-action style text, appropriate for use in buttons or links, guiding the customer on how to use the reward fulfillment. This field may be blank

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-action-url-one-of-0)

**reward_fulfillment.action_url**

**string | null**

A URL destination guiding the customer on how to use the reward, often paired with the `action_text` for use in buttons or links. This field may be blank.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-usage-instructions-one-of-0)

**reward_fulfillment.usage_instructions**

**string | null**

A description of how the customer can use or apply the reward fulfillment.

Example:

`"Use this discount code on your next order!"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-terms-and-conditions-one-of-0)

**reward_fulfillment.terms_and_conditions**

**string | null**

A description of the reward fulfillment's limitations or restrictions. When the reward is a discount code, this commonly includes expiry date, minimum order amounts, or applicability to specific product collections.

Example:

`"Reward expires on June 7, 2025. Reward can only be used on purchase of $50 or more. Reward can only be used on select product collections."`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-expires-at-one-of-0)

**reward_fulfillment.expires_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment expires. When the reward is a discount code, this refers to the expiry date of the discount, and a `null` value indicates that the discount code does not expire.

Example:

`"2025-06-07T23:59:59.999Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-usage-status)

**reward_fulfillment.usage_status**

**enum\<string\>**

The usage status of the reward fulfillment, indicating whether the generated reward has been used by the customer.

Available options:

`used`, 

`unused`, 

`untracked` 

Example:

`"unused"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-used-at-one-of-0)

**reward_fulfillment.used_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment was used by the customer. If the reward fulfillments does not support usage tracking, this field may be `null` even after the reward fulfillment has been used.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-created-at)

**reward_fulfillment.created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment was created.

Example:

`"2024-12-07T20:15:27.893Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-reward-fulfillment-updated-at)

**reward_fulfillment.updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward fulfillment was last updated.

Example:

`"2024-12-07T20:15:27.893Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-created-at)

**created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the points purchase was created.

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/points-purchases/points-purchase-object#schema-updated-at)

**updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the points purchase was last updated.

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`
