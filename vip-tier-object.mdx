---
title: "VIP Tier Object"
---

A VIP tier represents a status level that a customer achieves within Yuko's loyalty program. Tiers are based on a `threshold_value` (e.g. points accumulated) and reward customers with a points earning `multiplier` and a set of `benefits` such as discounts or free shipping.

**id**

**integer**

Unique integer identifier for the VIP tier.

Example:

`1`

**uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

Unique UUID identifier for the VIP tier.

Example:

`"7fb39c85-3a3f-4247-8333-2e05a9c8ae28"`

**org_uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

The UUID of the organisation this tier belongs to.

Example:

`"ed39de7b-6c31-46bc-af56-ef51676a08e0"`

**name**

**string**

The display name of the VIP tier.

Example:

`"Silver"`

**description**

**string | null**

A description of the VIP tier shown to customers.

Example:

`null`

**identifier**

**string**

A unique slug-style identifier for the tier, auto-generated from the tier name. Unique per organisation.

Example:

`"SILVER"`

**threshold_value**

**integer**

The points value a customer must accumulate to reach this tier.

Example:

`0`

**multiplier**

**number**

The points earning multiplier applied to customers in this tier. For example, `2` means customers earn double points on activities.

Example:

`1`

**is_default**

**boolean**

Whether this is the default tier assigned to customers who have not yet reached any threshold.

Example:

`false`

**benefits**

**object[]**

A list of benefits associated with this tier. Benefits are rewards or perks automatically issued to customers when they enter the tier.

Hide child attributes

**benefits.uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

Unique identifier for the benefit.

Example:

`"b1c2d3e4-f5a6-7890-bcde-f01234567890"`

**benefits.tier_uuid**

**string\<uuid\>**

The UUID of the tier this benefit belongs to.

Example:

`"7fb39c85-3a3f-4247-8333-2e05a9c8ae28"`

**benefits.name**

**string**

The display name of the benefit.

Example:

`"10% Discount on entry"`

**benefits.benefit_type**

**string**

The type of benefit issued to the customer.

Example:

`"fixed_discount"`

**benefits.benefit_value**

**object**

The value configuration for this benefit. Structure varies by `benefit_type`.

Show child attributes

**benefits.benefit_value.value**

**number**

The discount amount or percentage depending on `benefit_type`.

Example:

`10`

**benefits.benefit_value.points_required**

**integer**

Points required to redeem this benefit, if applicable.

Example:

`0`

**benefits.platform_discount_id**

**string | null**

The discount identifier generated in the external ecommerce platform for this benefit.

Example:

`"1121797341367"`

**benefits.ordering**

**integer**

The sort order of the benefit within the tier.

Example:

`0`

**benefits.metadata**

**object | null**

Additional metadata for the benefit.

Example:

`null`

**benefits.created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the benefit was created.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**benefits.updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the benefit was last updated.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**metadata**

**object | null**

Additional metadata for the tier.

Example:

`null`

**created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the tier was created.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the tier was last updated.

Example:

`"2025-09-12T15:44:44.000Z"`

**deleted_at**

**string\<date-time\> | null**

The date and time when the tier was deleted. `null` if not deleted.

Example:

`null`
