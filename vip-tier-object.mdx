---
title: "VIP Tier Object"
---

A VIP tier represents a status or level that a customer can achieve as they move through a VIP program. Customers move through different VIP tiers by accruing points or making purchases.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-id)

**id**

**integer**

Unique identifier for the VIP tier.

Example:

`426715799`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-name)

**name**

**string**

The display name of the VIP tier.

Example:

`"Gold"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-image-url)

**image_url**

**string**

The image of the VIP tier.

Example:

`"https://platform.smile.io/v1/images/vip_tiers/vip-tier-3.svg"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-milestone)

**milestone**

**integer**

The threshold that a customer must reach to be eligible for the VIP tier. Represents either an amount of points or a dollar amount, depending on the loyalty program's configuration.

Example:

`500`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-perks)

**perks**

**object[]**

A set of nested objects representing the ongoing benefits a customer is entitled to when they are in the VIP tier.

Hide child attributes

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-perks-items-name)

**perks.name**

**string**

A description of the benefit.

Example:

`"Early access to new product releases"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-entry-rewards)

**entry_rewards**

**object[]**

A set of nested Reward objects representing the rewards that are issued to a customer when they enter the VIP tier.

Hide child attributes

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-entry-rewards-items-id)

**entry_rewards.id**

**number**

Unique identifier for the reward.

Example:

`924565472`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-entry-rewards-items-name)

**entry_rewards.name**

**string**

The name of the reward.

Example:

`"Order discount"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-entry-rewards-items-description)

**entry_rewards.description**

**string**

A description of the reward.

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-entry-rewards-items-image-url)

**entry_rewards.image_url**

**string**

An image for the reward.

Example:

`"https://platform-images.smilecdn.co/3755938.png"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-entry-rewards-items-created-at)

**entry_rewards.created_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward was created.

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`

[**​**](https://dev.smile.io/api/resources/vip-tiers/vip-tier-object#schema-entry-rewards-items-updated-at)

**entry_rewards.updated_at**

**string\<date-time\>**

The date and time when the reward was last updated.

Example:

`"2024-04-04T15:10:42.030Z"`
